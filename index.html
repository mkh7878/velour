Velour is a music project founded by songwriter Mae Horak in London in 2022 and currently based in Southern California.

Velour’s songs range from the cinematic to the surreal with a through line of 90s British rock. 

Mae has performed solo shows in London and Manchester in the UK as well as in major US cities including Chicago, Boston and San Diego. She also sometimes performs with a full band.
